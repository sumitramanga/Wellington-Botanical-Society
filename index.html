Help Advocate The Conservation And Protection Of New Zealand Native Plants

Wellington Botanical Society, established in 1939, has membership of approximately 280 amateur and professional botanists.

Our activities include research, field work, publication, advocacy, and the provision of botanical expertise. We support botanical research and endeavour through grants and advice to students.

We welcome vistors of all kind to our meetings and field trips.
